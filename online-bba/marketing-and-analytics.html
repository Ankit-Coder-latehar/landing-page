ABOUT ONLINE BBA IN MARKETING AND ANALYTICS

BBA Electives: Marketing & Analytics

The BBA in Marketing and Analytics at Vignan Online integrates strategic marketing fundamentals with cutting-edge analytical tools, enabling learners to analyze customer trends, measure campaign performance, and drive data-backed business growth.

Marketing Specialization:
Focuses on modern consumer behavior, brand management, digital channel strategies, e-commerce, customer relationship management (CRM), and global market dynamics. Students gain practical knowledge on structuring campaigns and communicating brand value effectively.

Analytics Specialization:
Empowers learners with essential analytics skills including data visualization, web & social media metrics, predictive analytics, consumer insights, and business intelligence tools. This dual edge helps students transform raw market data into actionable strategic insights.
Through real-world case studies and industry-curated projects, students develop the skills required for high-demand business roles in today's tech-driven corporate landscape.

Program credits: 120

Eligibility Criteria

FAQs

1. What is the Online BBA in Marketing and Analytics program?
The Online BBA in Marketing and Analytics is a 3-year undergraduate degree program that combines core business management, digital marketing strategies, consumer insights, and data analytics methods to prepare students for modern data-driven corporate roles.
2. What career options are available after completing BBA in Marketing and Analytics?
Graduates can work in prominent roles such as Marketing Analyst, Digital Marketing Specialist, Business Intelligence Consultant, Brand Strategist, Growth Analyst, and Market Research Specialist across top IT, FMCG, E-commerce, and consulting firms.
3. Is this Online BBA degree recognized by UGC and AICTE?
Yes, Vignan’s Online BBA program is UGC-Entitled and offered by a NAAC A+ Graded University, making the degree fully recognized for higher studies, government examinations, and corporate employment worldwide.
4. Are live interactive weekend classes available?
Yes, live interactive sessions are scheduled on weekends to allow working individuals and students to study conveniently while balancing work and personal commitments. Recorded lectures are also archived on the LMS.
