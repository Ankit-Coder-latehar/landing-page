ABOUT ONLINE BCA IN DATA SCIENCE

BCA Elective: Data Science & Analytics

The Online BCA in Data Science program at Vignan Online equips learners with essential computer science principles alongside specialized data analytics and artificial intelligence competencies. Students build solid foundations in programming, database systems, statistical analysis, and machine learning.

Data Science Elective Highlights:
Focuses on statistical modeling, data visualization, predictive analytics, machine learning algorithms, Python, SQL, and big data technologies (Spark, Hadoop). Learners develop hands-on expertise in manipulating large datasets, building machine learning models, and extracting actionable business intelligence.
Through industry-relevant case studies, cloud coding labs, and a final capstone project, graduates prepare for rewarding roles as Data Analysts, Data Engineers, Machine Learning Associates, and Business Intelligence Specialists.

Program credits: 120

Eligibility Criteria

Pass in the (10+2) examination from State Board / CBSE / NIOS / IGCSE / IB / ICSE recognized by the State or Central Government with a minimum aggregate of 50% or an equivalent letter/numerical grade and must have Mathematics / Computer Science / Information Technology / Information Practice as one of the subjects. A relaxation of 5% shall be given to SC/ST candidates. Candidates due to appear in 10+2 examination are also eligible to apply.

FAQs

1. What is the Online BCA in Data Science program?
The Online BCA in Data Science is a 3-year undergraduate degree program combining core computer applications with specialized subjects in data analytics, Python programming, machine learning, SQL databases, and big data visualization.
2. What career roles can I apply for after BCA in Data Science?
Graduates can work as Data Analyst, Machine Learning Associate, Business Intelligence Analyst, Data Engineer, Data Science Consultant, and Software Developer across top MNCs and IT firms.
3. Is this degree UGC Entitled and recognized globally?
Yes, Vignan’s Online BCA program is UGC-Entitled and offered by a NAAC A+ Graded University, making the degree fully recognized for higher studies, government exams, and global employment.
4. Are live weekend classes conducted?
Yes, live interactive sessions take place on weekends so students and working professionals can conveniently attend classes and resolve doubts with expert faculty.
