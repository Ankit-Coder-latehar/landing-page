ABOUT ONLINE MBA IN MARKETING

MBA Elective: Marketing Management

The Online MBA in Marketing specialization at Vignan Online prepares high-potential professionals to formulate strategic marketing visions, drive customer acquisitions, manage global brand equity, and leverage digital technology to outpace competitors.

Marketing Elective Focus:
Delves into strategic brand management, digital marketing & social media campaigns, consumer psychology & behavior analytics, international marketing strategy, retail management, sales & distribution optimization, and customer relationship management (CRM).
Using real-world Harvard case studies and live interactive weekend masterclasses, students gain high-impact managerial capabilities required for C-suite and senior leadership marketing roles.

Program credits: 90

FAQs

1. What is the Online MBA in Marketing program?
The Online MBA in Marketing is a 2-year postgraduate program that covers core managerial disciplines alongside specialized modules in brand strategy, digital marketing, consumer insights, sales management, and CRM analytics.
2. What career roles are available after completing MBA in Marketing?
Graduates can apply for prominent positions such as Marketing Manager, Brand Manager, Digital Marketing Lead, Product Manager, Business Development Manager, and Growth Strategist.
3. Is the degree UGC Entitled and valid for corporate hiring?
Yes, Vignan's Online MBA is UGC-Entitled and accredited with NAAC A+, ensuring full recognition for private sector jobs, government competitive exams, and global employment.
4. How are live classes organized for working professionals?
Live sessions take place on weekends, and recorded lectures are available 24/7 on our LMS so you can learn flexibly without hampering your current work schedule.
